OUR
STORY

ESTD : 2021

ZippMat was born out of a bold vision: to eliminate delays, inefficiencies, and high costs in supply chains – while delivering innovative, eco-conscious products made from repurposed materials.

What began as a small team has grown into a dynamic group of 50+ professionals, all driven by a shared mission: Faster deliveries. Smarter systems. Lower costs. Greener alternatives.
