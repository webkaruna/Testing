Our Products

VG40
VG40 bitumen has a high viscosity and is widely utilised in hot weather applications. It has a higher viscosity than VG30, making it more resistant to rutting and distortion at hot temperatures.
VG30
VG30 is a bitumen grade with a viscosity of 3000 centistokes at 60 degrees Celsius. It is a versatile bitumen grade that can be used for a range of applications such as roadways, pavements, and parking lots.
VG10
The viscosity grade of VG10 bitumen is used to classify it. VG10 bitumen has a low viscosity and is often used in cold conditions.
Crumb Rubber Modified Bitumen 60/40
Crumb rubber modified bitumen (CRMB) is a form of bitumen that has been treated using crumb rubber. CRMB is a sustainable product that can assist to reduce the amount of garbage that ends up in landfills.
Polymer Modified Bitumen
Polymer modified bitumen (PMB) is a form of bitumen that has been polymerized. It is also less prone to cracking and rutting, making it an excellent choice for high-traffic areas.
Bitumen Emulsion
Bitumen emulsion is a water-based solution made from bitumen, water, and emulsifying agents. It is a versatile material that can be used for a range of purposes such as paving, patching, and sealing.
