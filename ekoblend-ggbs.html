EkoBlend GGBS

EkoBlend, a premium brand from ZippMat, offers the best quality GGBS (Ground Granulated Blast Furnace Slag) - a sustainable alternative to traditional cement which enhances strength, durability, and making it an ideal choice for eco-conscious and future-ready construction.

for Sustainable Construction

Recycles Industrial Waste
Instead of ending up in landfills, slag is repurposed into high-performance concrete, promoting a circular economy.
Preserves Natural Resources
Decreases the amount of raw quarried limestone extracted, preserving natural resources.
Lowers Energy Consumption
Drastically lowers energy consumption, requiring approximately 80% less energy to produce than a ton of Portland cement.
Reduces Carbon Footprint
GGBS is a byproduct of steel manufacturing, reducing reliance on energy-intensive cement and cutting CO₂ emissions.

This ensures our customers receive a product that not only enhances the strength and durability of concrete but also meets the highest benchmarks of quality and consistency set by the Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS).
